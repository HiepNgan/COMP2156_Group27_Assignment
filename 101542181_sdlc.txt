Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

SDLC is a stucctured process used to devop high-quality software. It consists of different phases such as planning,analysis, design, implementation, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
There are different SDLC models including:
- Waterfall Model
- Agile Model
-DevOps Model

The Waterfall model follows a sequential approach, while Agile focuses on interative development and collaboration.

DevOps extends SDLC by integrating development and operations teams and emphasizing automation, continuous feedback, and faster delivery cycles.
